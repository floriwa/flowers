Camellia

Camellia is a genus of flowering plants in the family Theaceae.
They are found in eastern and southern Asia, from the
Himalayas east to Japan and Indonesia.

There are 100-300 described species, with some controversy over
the exact number. There are also around 3,000 hybrids.
